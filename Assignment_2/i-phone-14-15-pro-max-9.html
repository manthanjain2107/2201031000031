Vishveshwar

Kashi Vishwanath Temple is a hindu

Temple dedicated to Shiva. It is located

in Vishwanath Gali, in Varansi, 

uttar pradesh, India. The temple is a 

Hindu piligram site and is one of

the twelve jyotirlinga shrines. The

presiding deity is known by the names

Vishwanath and Vishweshwara

(IAST: Viśvanātha and Viśveśvara),

literally meaning Lord of the Universe.
