Omkareshwar

Omkareshwar is a hindu Tamples

dedicated to Shiva, located in 

Mandhata, nearby Khandwa City in 

Khandwa district of the India state

of Madhya pradesh.

 It is one of the 12 revered jyotirlingas 

shrines of Shiva.It is on an island

called Mandhata, near Khandwa city 

in the Narmada river at Khandwa

district in Madhya pradesh,

India; the shape of the island is said

to be like the Devanagari ॐ  symbol.
