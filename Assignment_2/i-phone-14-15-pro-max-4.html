Sri Bhramaramba Mallikarjuna Temple

or Srisailam Temple is a Hindu temple 

dedicated to the deities Shiva and 

Parvati, located at Srisailam in the 

Indian state of Andhra pradesh. 

It is significant to the Hindu sects of

both Shaivism and Shaktism as this

temple is referred to as one of the

twelve Jyotirlingas of Shiva and as

one of the eighteen Sshakti pithas, 

Shiva is worshiped as Mallikarjuna,

and is represented by the Lingam.

His consort Parvati is depicted as

Bhramrambha.
