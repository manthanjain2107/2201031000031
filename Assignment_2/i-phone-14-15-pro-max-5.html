Mahakaleshwar

Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga is

a Hindus temple dedicated to Shiva 

and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas,

shrines which are said to be the most

sacred abodes of Shiva.

It is located in the ancient city of

Ujjain in the state of Madhya Pradesh, 

India.

The temple is situated on the side of

the holy river Shipra. The presiding

deity.
