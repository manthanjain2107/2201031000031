Trayambakeshwar

The Shri Trimbakeshwar Shiva Temple

 is an ancient Hindu tample in the

town of Trimbak, in the Trimbakeshwar

tehsil in the Nashik district of 

Maharastra, India, 28 km from the

city of Nashik and 40 km from Nashik

road. It is dedicated to the Hindu

god Shiva and is one of the twelve 

Jyotirlinga where the Hindu genealogy

registers at Trimbakeshwar,

Maharastra are kept. The origin

of the sacred Godawari river is near

Trimbak.
